About Me

I first got into Web Design as I was entering highschool. I was active on websites like deviantArt and tumblr which allowed users to customize their profile pages through the use of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I went on to take classes at my local community college for a few years before going away to a university to hone my skills further. I enjoy helping friends create and debug their own coding projects, be it personal websites or designing snippets for websites they frequent.

In my free time I write stories, draw, and play videogames. I'm active in online art communities by taking commissions, hosting contests, and participating in group events. I'd like to create a small game of my own one day.

Current Projects

Reflexology

Part of a web-based, virtual pets game combining elements from Neopets, Harvest Moon, and Dungeons & Dragons. This section involves a daily click event used to optain materials, seeds, and bait for crafting tools, gardening, and fishing respectively. Both gardening and fishing are done through idle gameplay.
